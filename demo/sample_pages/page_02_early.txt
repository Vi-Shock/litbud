The Tortoise and the Hare
(From Aesop's Fables — public domain)

The hare was very fast and the tortoise was very slow.
One day, the hare said, "Let us have a race!"
The tortoise said, "Yes, I will race you."

The hare ran so fast that he sat down to rest.
He fell asleep under a big tree.
The tortoise walked slowly but he never stopped.
When the hare woke up, the tortoise had won!

Slow and steady wins the race.
