The Secret Garden
(Excerpt adapted from Frances Hodgson Burnett, 1911 — public domain)

Mary Lennox had always been a sickly, unhappy child.
When she came to live at Misselthwaite Manor, she found a mysterious locked door in the garden wall.
She searched and searched until she discovered a hidden key buried in the earth.

Behind the door was a secret garden that nobody had entered for ten years.
The branches were twisted and the flowers had disappeared under weeds.
But deep in the soil, something wonderful was still alive.

Mary decided she would make the garden grow again.
