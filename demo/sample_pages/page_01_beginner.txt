The Three Little Pigs
(Adapted from traditional English folk tale — public domain)

Once upon a time, three little pigs left home.
The first pig built a house of straw.
The second pig built a house of sticks.
The third pig built a house of bricks.

A big bad wolf came to the straw house.
"Let me in!" said the wolf.
"No!" said the pig.
The wolf huffed and he puffed.
He blew the house down.
